About Planet Enviro

Planet Enviro Services, Pune, is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company offering end-to-end environmental solutions. We specialize in EIA studies, regulatory clearances, pollution control, waste management, and water & wastewater treatment.

From consultancy to execution and monitoring, we are a one-stop partner helping organizations minimize their ecological footprint and move towards greener, cleaner growth

Our vision is simple: to protect our planet today and create a sustainable tomorrow for the next generation. 🌱
